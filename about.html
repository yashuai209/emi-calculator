About EMI Calculator

Welcome to EMI Calculator — a free, accurate, and user-friendly online tool that helps you calculate monthly EMIs, total interest, and total loan repayment with ease. Whether you're planning a personal loan, car loan, or home loan, this tool provides quick and precise results to help you make better financial decisions.

Our EMI Calculator uses a standard EMI formula, ensuring the most accurate calculations possible. We value user privacy — no data is stored or tracked. This tool is designed to be lightweight, mobile-friendly, and accessible on all devices.

Developed and maintained by Yashwant Khutte in India, this project aims to make financial planning simple and accessible to everyone.
